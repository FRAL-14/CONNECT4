WHAT IS CONNECT-4 ?

Connect4 is a two-player connection board game, in which the players are assigned a symbol (X or O) and take turns dropping it into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid.

The pieces fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column.

The objective of the game is to be the first to form an horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own symbols.

Connect Four is a solved game.
